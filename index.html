CODA-AI: Today and the Vision

Today, CODASOL combines industrial data experience, MDM delivery knowledge, and domain understanding from asset-intensive operating environments. The vision for CODA-AI is to convert that foundation into a scalable vertical AI-MDM intelligence layer for classification, enrichment, governance, deduplication, asset data quality, and MDM decision support.

CODA-AI is designed to sit above existing enterprise systems and support them; it does not replace SAP, Oracle, IBM, Microsoft systems, or legacy platforms.
